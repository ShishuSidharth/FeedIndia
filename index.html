What we do

We aim to build a community of people who want to join us in our aim to make a hunger free India. Here we will have people who will give an alert about extra food at Parties, Restaurants, gatherings etc. which is actually going to be wasted . So they will provide the information about the food available and the location on our website. Our volunteers who are nearby will reach out there, collect the food, divide them into packets, and distribute among needy ones.
We are not a charity organisation we aim to build a community of people who wish to help others and stop wastage of food when there are so many people dying from hunger.
